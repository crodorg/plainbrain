# People — trust roster

Authors and figures whose views the wiki tracks, each with a **tier** setting how much weight
their claims carry when sources conflict. Source pages link here via `by:` and inherit the
author's tier; `wiki-query` weighs conflicting claims by tier and names whose view each is.

## Tiers

- **T1 — trusted**: domain authority. Strong prior, still falsifiable.
- **T2 — credible**: generally reliable; weigh normally.
- **T3 — unweighted**: unknown or unvetted; report the claim, lean on corroboration.

Tune the tiers to your own trust model — they are a convention, not a schema.
